Animal Facts!

Bactrian Camel

  1. Camels chew in a figure 8 moition.
  2. When they feel threatened camels spit.
  3. They can go six months without eating.
  4. They can drink up to 5 gallons in one sitting.
  5. Bactrian Camels only have one hump.

Betta

  1. Every betta has a different tail shape.
  2. Bettas are extremely territorial.
  3. They live in tropical waters.
  4. There average lifespan is five years.
  5. If two bettas fight it usually leads to serious injury but it can sometimes lead to death.

Kangaroos

  1. Male Kangaroos fight over the females by boxing eachother out.
  2. They watch there baby kangaroos for only about 7 months.
  3. Baby kangaroos are called joeys.
  4. They are mammals
  5. They are marsupials
